Research

Research domains and projects that reflect our commitment to general, scalable, and efficient AI.

Research Interest

AI Principles: Study the fundamental structure and learning mechanisms of modern AI models.

Scalability & Efficiency: Design architectures and training strategies for scalable and efficient AI.

Generalization: Develop AI systems that adapt across domains, tasks, and data modalities.

Foundation Model: Research large-scale models from a core architectural and training perspective.

Academic Sustainability: Make large models trainable even with limited resources compared to industry.

Project

SAILab is active in projects that bring academic AI into practice. We build scalable training workflows for university research, create adaptive multi-domain systems that generalize beyond specific tasks, and analyze foundation models to understand their architecture and optimization. We also explore efficient academic AI methods that reduce resource demand while maintaining strong performance.
