SAILab investigates the core foundations of AI systems and develops methods that make state-of-the-art models more general, scalable, and efficient.

Foundation ModelExplore large-scale models from a core architectural and training perspective.
Academic SustainabilityMake large models trainable even with limited resources compared to industry.
