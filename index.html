From Principle to Practice

Building General, Scalable, and Efficient AI

SAILab explores AI research from theory to implementation. We develop models and systems that are general, scalable, efficient, and sustainable in academic settings.

Research Interest

SAILab focuses on five core research areas that shape our approach to modern AI:

  • AI Principles
  • Scalability & Efficiency
  • Generalization
  • Foundation Model
  • Academic Sustainability

We study the principles behind learning systems, design architectures for efficient scale, enable robust adaptation across domains, investigate foundation models from a structural perspective, and make large model research accessible with limited resources.
