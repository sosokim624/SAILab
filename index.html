Research Focus

SAILab explores the core principles of AI by integrating theory with practical experiments. Our focus is on building systems that are general, scalable, and efficient while remaining suited to academic research environments.

We investigate AI Principles, Scalability & Efficiency, Generalization, Foundation Models, and Academic Sustainability as a unified research agenda.

Research Interest

At SAILab, our research interest stretches across multiple dimensions. AI Principles focuses on the basic structure and learning dynamics behind modern AI systems. Scalability & Efficiency is about designing architectures and training strategies that work at scale without waste. Generalization is concerned with creating models that adapt across domains, tasks, and data modalities. Foundation Model research examines large-scale systems from a core architectural and training perspective. Academic Sustainability seeks ways to make large models trainable with limited resources compared to industry.
